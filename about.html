Our story

Relatively new to the underground scene, Native first emerged in Brighton late in 2016 as part of a project seeking to spread the love for underground house, techno, and disco.

The King Road arches of the sunny seaside town of Brighton are etched firmly into the UK dance scene and were home to our first after hour resident parties. Our parties tend to carry on well past the twilight hours, and when the sun rises over the South Coast it creates a magical experience. An experience at a Native event instills a sense of belonging and we welcome you to join our next event.

From the second we open our doors our philosophy is simple: to ensure your experience with us makes you one with the music.
